The Dark Passenger

Orphaned at age three after witnessing the brutal murder of his mother, Dexter Morgan was adopted by Miami police officer Harry Morgan. Recognizing the boy's sociopathic tendencies, Harry taught Dexter to channel his inner "Dark Passenger" into a constructive pursuit: killing only heinous criminals who have escaped the legal system.


Dexter follows "The Code of Harry," which ensures he never kills an innocent person and meticulously covers his tracks to avoid detection. He balances his everyday life as a blood-spatter analyst for Miami Metro with his nocturnal activities as a vigilante.

The Series Chronicle

Season 1 (2006)

Dexter meets the "Ice Truck Killer," a serial killer who knows Dexter's secret and challenges him to remember his suppressed past.

Season 2 (2007)

Dexter's dumping ground is discovered, leading to the "Bay Harbor Butcher" investigation by the FBI and his own department.

Season 3 (2008)

Dexter forms an unlikely friendship with Assistant DA Miguel Prado, who discovers Dexter's secret and wants to join in.

Season 4 (2009)

Dexter balances fatherhood while hunting the "Trinity Killer," a master serial killer who has operated for 30 years.

Season 5 (2010)

Dexter finds an ally in Lumen Pierce, a victim of a barrel stuffing ring, and helps her seek vengeance against her tormentors.

Season 6 (2011)

Dexter confronts the "Doomsday Killer," a religious zealot performing ritualistic murders based on the Book of Revelation.

Season 7 (2012)

Debra discovers Dexter's secret, complicating their relationship as they face the Koshka Brotherhood mob.

Season 8 (2013)

The final chapter involving the "Brain Surgeon" and the creator of Harry's Code, Dr. Evelyn Vogel. Dexter faces his ultimate fate.
